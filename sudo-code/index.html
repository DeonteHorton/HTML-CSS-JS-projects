How to Bake A Chocalate Cake!

Step one: Gather your ingredients

  • 3/4 cup (170 g) of unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup (64 g) of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3/4 cup (90 g) of flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon (1.4 g) of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon (1.2 g) of baking powder
  • 1 cup (225 g) of granulated sugar
  • 3 eggs, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon (4.9 mL) of vanilla extract
  • 1⁄2 cup (120 mL) of buttermilk or sour cream

Step Two: Gather your Baking Tools

  1. Oven
  2. One large bowl
  3. One Small bowl
  4. Measuring cups and spoons
  5. Electric mixer
  6. Pastry brush
  7. Toppings
  8. Frosting
  9. Cake filling
  10. Pastry bag
  11. Pan of Choice: round standard cake pan, a square baking dish, a loaf pan,, or whatever you have on hand

Step Three: Oven Prep

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (177 °C)
  2. Get your pan, Grease the pan well with butter to keep the cake from sticking to it.
  3. After greasing, add an even layer of flour inside the pan.

Step Four: Mix Ingredients in a Large Bowl

Important!! Place moist and dry ingrediens in separate bowl

  1. Moist Ingredients - Place the butter, vanilla extract, sugar, and buttermilk in a bowl, crack the eggs and place it in the same bowl, then use a hand or stand mixer to blend the ingredients well.
  2. Dry Ingredients - Place the the flour, salt, cocoa powder, and baking powder in a small bowl. Stir them together
  3. Add the dry mixture to the wet mixture slowly.
  4. Beat the mixture on low with electric mixer until the batter comes together and no white bits of flour remain
  5. Turn off the electric mixer

Step Five: Place Mixture into the pan and place in the oven

  1. Pour the batter into the cake pan. Use a spoon or spatula to scrape the sides of the bowl so every bit of batter makes it into the pan.
  2. Place the pan in the oven for 30 minutes
  3. At 15 minutes, Rotate the pan 180 degress to ensure it's cooking evenly
  4. Check the cake often to ensure the cake doesn't burn.

Step Six: Cooling process

  1. Remove the cake out the oven, turn the oven off.
  2. Let it sit in a cooling rack for 5 minutes before handling it
  3. Place the cake on the plate that your are serving with
  4. Ensure the cake is completely cooled before applying frosting!!

Step Seven: Apply Frosting to cake

  1. Make some chocolate buttercream frosting or plain buttercream frosting, or any other kind of frosting
  2. Apply frosting to the cake
  3. Enjoy the cake
  4. Yell "YUM!!".
